The Programme

What you will learn

01

Neapolitan Pizza

From kneading the dough to baking in the wood-fired oven. Claudio's secrets for a perfect pizza.

02

Fresh Pasta

Hand-made Gnocchi alla Sorrentina and Ravioli Capresi, from the dough to the filling.

03

Aubergine Parmigiana

The great Campanian classic: aubergine, tomato, mozzarella and basil in the wood-fired oven.

04

Tiramisù

The classic coffee version and the limoncello one — a Sorrento speciality.

05

Limoncello

Sorrento's lemons are world-famous. Discover how authentic artisan limoncello is made.

06

Tasting & View

Savour everything you prepared on the panoramic terrace overlooking the Gulf of Naples. Wine included.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Is culinary experience required? ⌄
Absolutely not. Our experiences are suitable for all levels. Our chefs guide every participant step by step.
Is hotel pickup available? ⌄
Yes, on request from central Sorrento hotels. You can also make your own way to Via Casarlano 15, 80067 Sorrento (NA). Parking is available.
Are the experiences suitable for children? ⌄
Yes, our experiences are family-friendly. The venue is wheelchair and stroller accessible.
How does cancellation and refund work? ⌄
100% refund if you cancel by 8:00 PM the day before your experience. Refund processed within 5-10 business days. No refund after this deadline.
I have food allergies — can I still join? ⌄
Please note any allergies or intolerances in the notes field when booking. Our team will do their best to accommodate you.
Can I book for a private group? ⌄
Yes! For private classes or exclusive groups write to prenotazioni@anticofrantoiosorrento.it. We organise bespoke sessions.

Ready to step into the kitchen?

Book directly above. Places are limited to 20 guests per class.
